=== B>64 Correctness Probe Results (Zen4, 2025-07-22) ===

Tested hybrid engine forced at B ∈ {8,16,24,32,48,64,96,128,192,256}
against V1 exact reference, across all verification test cases:
n ∈ {16,32,64,128,256,1024,4096,16384,65536}
distributions: uniform, adversarial, geometric, equal, adv_1e9

RESULT: ALL TESTS PASSED at every B value.
- Standard tolerance (5e-12): all errors ≤ 1.18e-12 (well within bound)
- Adversarial tolerance (1e-9): all errors ≤ 1.54e-10 (well within bound)
- Zero degradation with B increase — errors are flat from B=8 through B=256
- No sudden blowup, no gradual drift
- The adversarial (S[0]=10000, rest=1) case, which is the near-a_j=0.5
  regime the paper's lemma flags as riskiest, shows max error ~5e-13 at all B

CONCLUSION: B up to 256 is empirically correct on Zen4 hardware.
select_best_B()'s candidate ceiling of 64 is conservative but not
required for correctness. Upward revision depends on the analytical
FP64-epsilon-budget derivation (separate task).
