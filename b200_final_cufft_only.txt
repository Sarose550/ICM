=== VkFFT DECISION ===
VkFFT was evaluated separately (vkfft_comparison.csv, 736 sizes tested):
  cuFFT wins: 331 sizes
  VkFFT wins: 357 sizes
  Ties (<1%): 48 sizes
Net effect on ICM: 1-2% regression. The dual-dispatch approach (per-size
library selection like CPU FFTW/MKL) could recover VkFFT wins at select
sizes, but the overhead of gather/scatter kernels (VkFFT requires contiguous
layout, cuFFT works with strided batches) eliminates the gain since most
tree levels share the same FFT size.

DECISION: Ship cuFFT-only. VkFFT integration is verified working but not
worth enabling by default on B200 hardware.
